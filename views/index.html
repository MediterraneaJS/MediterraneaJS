About the Event

MediterráneaJS is a 2-day not-for-profit by-the-community for-the-community international conference about JavaScript. It is organised by BarcelonaJS.

With MediterráneaJS we want to inspire, much like the city of Barcelona. That's why the event is not happening in some boring, flat and simple conference center somewhere out of the city, but in one of the most 'fashionista' neighborhoods: 'El Born', the very center of Barcelona. We want to you explore, get lost, refresh and find yourself, in the talks as much as in Barcelona. To take home more than just a little bit more JavaScript.
