About the Event

MediterráneaJS is a 2-day not-for-profit by-the-community for-the-community international conference about JavaScript organised by BarcelonaJS.

Much like the city of Barcelona, with MediterráneaJS we want to inspire. That's why the event is not happening in some boring, flat and simple conference center somewhere out of the city, but in one of the most 'fashionista' neighborhoods - 'El Born' - in the very center of Barcelona. We want you to explore, get lost, refresh and find yourself, in the talks as much as in Barcelona. Prepare to take home more than just a little bit more JavaScript.

We've compiled some information on how to get to Barcelona, where to stay and what to do.

MediterráneaJS is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. Please read our code of conduct.
